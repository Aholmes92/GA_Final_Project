Welcome to Ellie's Oils!

Ellie's Oils is an Artisan Extra Virgin Olive Oil and
Balsamic Vinegar tasting room located in
Northampton, Massachusetts.

We offer a wide selection of Ultra Premium extra virgin olive oil from around the world and aged balsamic vinegar from Modena, Italy. At Ellie's Oil's unique tasting bar you have the opportunity to taste all of the finest Ultra Premium extra virgin olive oils as well as all of the naturally fused and infused olive oils and the variety of dark and white balsamic vinegars we offer. This allows our customer the ability to craft unique blends of extra virgin olive oil and aged balsamic vinegars to their tastes. The oils and vinegars are then individually bottled and sealed on-site for purchase.


To ensure ultimate freshness of our olive oils, Ellie's Oils and our importer partner work with growers in Northern and Southern hemispheres to identify the latest harvests. We call this 'chasing the crush' and it guarantees that we offer the freshest Ultra Premium extra virgin olive oil around. All of our olive oils are 100% extra virgin, unfiltered and settled.

Read More...

All of Ellie's Oils' balsamic vinegars are produced in Modena, Italy in the traditional style. This is a process of transferring the cooked grape must through a series of large wooden barrels for up to 18 years. We offer the traditional style, as well as flavors infused with dried fruit, fruit juices and natural flavors. All of our vinegars are caramel color-free, free of added sugar or thickeners and all natural.


Ellie's Oils is the brainchild of Barbara Goldstein and Matthew Wirth. Barbara discovered the wonderful world of olive oil and balsamic vinegar tasting rooms a few years ago while on vacation, and always yearned to have one close to home. Matthew, a long time chef, was looking for a change of pace after his daughter (and Barbara's grandaughter), Ellie was born in December. Both Barbara and Matthew were lured in by the freshest, most intensely flavored olive oils and balsamic vinegars they had ever tasted. Give them a try, Barbara and Matthew think you'll agree.

Lemon Fused Garden Salad w/ Chicken

Ingredients
  • 1/2 Head of lettuce
  • 1 Tomato
  • 1/2 Red onion
  • 1 Cup grilled chicken (chopped)
  • Lemon Fused Olive Oil
Directions

Cut head of lettuce, tomato, and onion and mix into a large bowl. Add grilled chicken and dress with Lemon Fused Olive Oil. Great for a quick healthy lunch or serving a large group.

Basil Flatbread Pizza

Ingredients
  • 1 Flatbread crust
  • 1/2 Tomato sauce
  • Pepperoni
  • Black olives)
  • 1 Cup mozzarella cheese
  • Basil Olive Oil
Directions

Layer flatbread evenly with Basil Olive Oil. Spread tomato sauce with spoon. Add desired amount of pepperoni and black olives. Sprinkle cheese on top. Place in oven at 405 degrees for 20 minutes. Enjoy!

Tuscan Pasta w/ Clams

Ingredients
  • Spaghetti
  • 1 Can diced tomatos
  • 1/4 lb Clams
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Tuscan Herb Infused Olive Oil
Directions

Cook spaghetti al dente. Drain. In medium pot, cook clams in diced tomatos for 20 mins, add to spaghetti when finished. Top with 2 Tbsp of Tuscan Herb Infused Olive Oil. Add dash of salt & pepper, serve hot on large dish. Mmm mm mm...
